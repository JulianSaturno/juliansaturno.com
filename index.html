Solar Tracker

Over the course of my third year at university, our junior design project required us to work with photovoltaic systems. We were interested in creating power-neutral systems able to identify optimal angles for maxiumum power output on the solar cell. A major lesson taken away from this experience was "engineering design is an iterative process".

Raspberry Pi Photobooth

During my first exposure to coding languages, my final assessment was to incorporate hardware on the Raspberry Pi with Python. With the help of a seven-segment display, push button, and a Pi camera, I developed a photobooth that was able to stitch a collage of burst photos and upload the final picture to Twitter using an API.
